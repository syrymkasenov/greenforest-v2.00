About the program BR18574148 "Development of geographic information systems

and monitoring of environmental facilities"

Program purpose

Создание единой экосистемы сбора и обработки данных мониторинга атмосферного воздуха промышленных городов Казахстана. Создание алгоритма обработки больших данных, собираемых имеющимися системами мониторинга качества воздуха.

Program task

1. Analysis of long-term historical data on air pollution in industrial cities of Kazakhstan;

2. Determination of the list of priority pollutants (markers, the most dangerous in terms of impact on atmospheric air and characterizing the main industry) for inclusion in the information and analytical platform;

3. Collection and integration of heterogeneous metadata from various sources, including contact measurement data and remote sensing data;

4. Formation of an up-to-date database of atmospheric air quality based on archival materials and data from our own monitoring of atmospheric air quality;

5. Selection of big data analysis tools for the development of the analytical part of the platform;

6. Adaptation of models of transfer and transformation of impurities in the atmosphere to the conditions of the selected industrial city;

7. Data processing based on computationally efficient mathematical algorithms for combining models and observational data to identify pollution sources, including algorithms based on variational and ensemble approaches, coupled tasks and operator equations;

8. Analytical processing of big data based on data mining methods to identify hidden patterns and the development of an expert system using artificial intelligence methods and machine learning algorithms;

9. Analysis of historical data using the developed mathematical apparatus;

10. Identification and ranking of atmospheric air quality factors affecting the quality of life of the population;

11. Creation of a prototype of a unified digital information and analytical platform for support and decision-making in the field of regional environmental policy, based on recognized international indices for assessing the quality of the atmosphere;

12. Deployment of a network of mobile and stationary atmospheric air monitoring sensors for suspended particles PM2.5 and PM10;

13. Organization of a situational center for monitoring and forecasting air pollution in industrial cities.

This program is in line with the Strategy "Kazakhstan-2050" and the activities of the following documents:

1. The national project "Green Kazakhstan".

2. The Strategic Development Plan of the Republic of Kazakhstan until 2025.

3. The national project "Technological breakthrough through digitalization, science and innovation".

4. The state program "Digital Kazakhstan": Goals, objectives, target indicators and indicators of the results of the program, Direction 1. Digitalization of economic sectors.

5. Environmental Code of the Republic of Kazakhstan. ST RK BSI PD 8101-2016 "Smart cities. A guide to assessing the role of planning and development."
