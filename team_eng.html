Ecoservice-S LLP

ECOSERVICE-S LLP is an accredited subject of scientific and scientific-technical activity in the Republic of Kazakhstan. It has experience and background in research programs using its own databases and monitoring systems. The company has developed information and analytical systems for environmental monitoring and accounting of emissions into the environment. It has its own monitoring network for pollutants in the atmospheric air.

Akademset LLP

Akademset LLP provides services for hosting physical servers in its own data center, including installing the operating system and necessary software, as well as configuring, administering and maintaining servers. RSE Kazhydromet

RSE Kazhydromet is the leading environmental monitoring body in Kazakhstan. It carries out a wide range of activities, including quality control of air, water, soil, meteorological and radiation measurements, as well as monitoring of transboundary pollution. The RSE provides reliable information to protect the environment and ensure the sustainable development of the country.

Tel/Fax: 8 (7172) 79-83-80
